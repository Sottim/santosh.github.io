Namaste ! Introducing Modern Nepali Culture

I am Santosh (UG_24, Ashoka University) from Pokhara, Nepal. It is the most beautiful city in Nepal which attracts thousands of tourists each year. Located 220 miles away from Capital City, Kathmandu and is surrounded by hills and mountains.

Not only tourists are facinated with the natural beauty of Pokhara valley but are equally mesmerised by the Nepali cultural dress transformed with the modern styles and fashion.

Therefore, my aim is to make it available for us all without you having to travel a long distance and still have the luxury of wearing them at your comfort zone.

These unisex dresses can be worn any time throughout the year because they look and fit, cool during all through summers and winters.
